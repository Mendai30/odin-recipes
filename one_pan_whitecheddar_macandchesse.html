One Pan White Cheddar Mac and Chesse Recipe

This is a recipe for making great One Pan White Cheddar Mac and Chesse

"Description"

Directions Step1: Combine macaroni, milk, water, garlic salt, and salt in a Dutch oven on a stovetop over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook, stirring frequently so noodles don't stick to the pan, until noodles are soft and most of the liquid has been absorbed, 7 to 10 minutes. Step2: Remove pot from heat and stir in margarine and pepper until melted. Stir in Cheddar cheese and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese until melted. Step3: Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler. Step5: Top pasta with bread crumbs, remaining 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese, and bacon bits. Step6: Place Dutch oven under the broiler until topping is lightly browned and crispy, 1 to 3 minutes. Top with chives and serve immediately.

"Ingredients"

"Make sure to get right ingerdients"
  1. 16 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  2. 2 ½ cups milk
  3. 2 cups water
  4. 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  5. ½ teaspoon salt
  6. ¼ cup margarine
  7. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  8. 1 ½ cups shredded white Cheddar cheese
  9. ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese, divided
  10. ¼ cup panko bread crumbs
  11. ¼ cup real bacon bits
  12. 1 tablespoon snipped chives
